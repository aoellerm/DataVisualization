Data Feminization

Overview of Data Feminism

Data feminization, or data feminism, is an approach that integrates feminist principles into data science, aiming to challenge power structures and promote social justice through data practices. Data feminism is a framework developed by Catherine D'Ignazio and Lauren Klein, which emphasizes the importance of intersectional feminist thought in the field of data science.
